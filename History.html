A Glimpse into the Past

Discovering Nijenhuis Castle

Historical Significance: Nijenhuis Castle is one of the best-preserved manors in Overijssel, and its history dates back to the late Middle Ages. As you explore this enchanting site, you'll gain insights into the aristocratic past of the region and the cultural heritage of Heino.

Architectural Beauty: The castle's architecture is a splendid example of Dutch manor design, featuring beautiful Dutch gables, period-accurate interiors, and a captivating blend of historical and modern aesthetics.

Nijenhuis Castle Museum

Art and Culture: The Nijenhuis Castle Museum is renowned for its versatile collection, which includes paintings, drawings, sculptures, and applied arts from various periods and cultures. It's a place where art enthusiasts can explore a wide array of artistic expressions.

Sculpture Garden: The castle grounds feature a mesmerizing sculpture garden, housing more than a hundred sculptures from the collections of Museum de Fundatie. It's an open-air gallery that combines nature and art.
