Experience The Vibrant Spirit Of Heino Village

Nijenhuis Castle

Nijenhuis Castle is one of the best preserved manors in Overijssel. Located between the villages of Heino and Wijhe, it is surrounded by beautiful landscape. The history of Nijenhuis Castle begins in the late Middle Ages.

It offers a versatile collection includes paintings, drawings, sculptures and applied arts from many periods and cultures, not to forget the sculpture garden that contains more than a 100 sculptures from the collections of Museum de Fundatie.

Summer camp Heino

Summer camp Heino is the largest and most fun camp location for all types of youth groups. It contains diverse amount of facilities including: swimming pool, sports field, recreational lake, and a petting zoo

Sallands harvest festival

The Sallands Harvest Festival is a fun celebration that happens every year in the beautiful Salland region. It's a time for local farmers, artisans, and community members to come together and celebrate the harvest season. The festival is all about the area's agricultural traditions and has lots of cool things to do, like farmers' markets with fresh produce, craft exhibitions, live music, and yummy food made with the best ingredients of the season. Families and friends get together to have a great time and appreciate the farming history that keeps the Sallands community going strong.
